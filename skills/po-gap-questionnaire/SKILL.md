# PO Gap Questionnaire

Generate structured Product Owner questionnaires to fill gaps in requirements,
acceptance criteria, scope definitions, and business validations.

## What problem this solves

Past projects accumulate gaps: undocumented requirements, missing acceptance
criteria, unvalidated deliveries. The PO needs a systematic way to identify
and close these gaps — without requiring the Professor to write formal documents
from scratch.

## When to use

When Manu's audit identifies gaps in:
- Requirements (missing, ambiguous, untraceable)
- Acceptance criteria (not defined, not testable)
- Scope (not approved, not bounded)
- Business validation (delivered without PO sign-off)

## Input

A gap report from Manu's audit (`.araya/plan/spec/po-retroactive-audit.md`)
or any PO gap analysis.

## Output

A structured questionnaire saved to `.araya/plan/spec/po-questionnaire-{topic}.md`
with numbered questions ready for The Data Professor to answer.

## Questionnaire Format

```markdown
# PO Gap Questionnaire — {Topic}
**Generated by:** Manu (Product Owner)
**Date:** {date}
**Priority:** Critical | High | Medium
**Gap Reference:** {audit item ID}

---

## Background

{Context of the gap — what was built, what's missing, why it matters}

## Questions for The Data Professor

**Answer by responding with the question number and your answer.**

### Requirements

1. **{Specific question about what's needed}**
   - Current state: {what exists now}
   - Gap: {what's missing}
   - Example answer: "{what a good answer looks like}"

2. **{Next question}**
   ...

### Acceptance Criteria

3. **How should we verify that {feature} is complete?**
   - What would you check to confirm it works?

4. **What would make you reject {feature}?**
   - What specific failures would block approval?

### Scope & Boundaries

5. **What is explicitly OUT of scope for {feature}?**
   - Current understanding: {what was assumed}
   - Needs clarification

### Business Priority

6. **On a scale of P0 (blocks everything) to P3 (nice to have), what priority is this?**
   - Current assumption: {priority}
   - Needs confirmation

7. **What business objective does this support?**
   - Options: [1] Revenue growth [2] Cost reduction [3] Compliance [4] Customer retention [5] New market

---

## Instructions

1. Read each question
2. Respond by number: "1. {your answer}" or "/araya manu answer 1: {your answer}"
3. Manu will apply your answers to the official requirements/acceptance criteria
4. Once all questions are answered, Manu will produce the final approved document

## Status

- [ ] Q1 answered
- [ ] Q2 answered
- [ ] Q3 answered
- [ ] Q4 answered
- [ ] Q5 answered
- [ ] Q6 answered
- [ ] Q7 answered
- [ ] All answers applied to official artifacts
- [ ] Manu signed off
```

## Steps

1. Read the gap report to understand what's missing
2. For each high-priority gap, generate a questionnaire
3. Each question must be numbered (max 7 per questionnaire)
4. Each question must include context, current state, gap, and example answer
5. Present to The Data Professor: "I have N questions about {topic}. Ready for your answers."
6. As The Data Professor answers by number, fill in the questionnaire
7. After all questions are answered, apply answers to official artifacts
8. Mark the gap as RESOLVED with Manu's sign-off

## Rules

- Max 7 questions per questionnaire — more is overwhelming
- Every question must have an example answer — don't make The Data Professor guess format
- Questions must be numbered for easy response: "3. My answer"
- Current state and gap must be stated before the question — context matters
- After answers are applied, produce the formal artifact (requirements doc, AC doc)
- If a question cannot be answered now, mark it DEFERRED with a reason
- Questionnaire goes to `.araya/plan/spec/po-questionnaire-{topic}.md`
## Done Criteria

- [ ] All steps completed as specified
- [ ] Output validated against requirements
- [ ] Status reported with confidence score
- [ ] Evidence artifacts captured
