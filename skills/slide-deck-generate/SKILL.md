# Slide Deck Generate

Create professional presentation slide decks — training materials, conference
talks, executive briefings, and project updates — with consistent branding,
clear narrative, and visual impact.

## What problem this solves
Great ideas presented poorly lose their impact. This skill transforms technical
content into visually clear, narratively structured slide decks that engage
audiences and communicate effectively.

## When to use
When preparing conference talks, training sessions, executive presentations,
project reviews, or any content that needs visual presentation.

## Input
Content to present, audience, presentation format, time constraints, brand
guidelines.

## Output
A slide deck structure with content per slide, speaker notes, and visual
recommendations — ready for PowerPoint, Google Slides, or Reveal.js.

## Steps
1. Define purpose: inform, persuade, teach, or inspire?
2. Structure narrative with clear arc: problem → solution → evidence → call to action
3. Design each slide: one idea per slide, 3-5 bullet points max, visual over text
4. Apply brand consistency: MAHG colors, fonts, logo placement from Dorcas
5. Add visuals: diagrams (architecture-diagram skill), charts, icons, screenshots
6. Write speaker notes: what to say beyond what's on screen
7. Practice timing: 1-2 minutes per slide for presentations

## Rules
- One idea per slide — if you need two bullets to explain, it's two slides
- 3-5 bullet points max per slide; 6 words per bullet max
- Visual over text: diagram, chart, or image on every slide
- Brand consistency: colors, fonts, logo per my-brands-guidelines
- Copyright footer on every slide: "© 2026 Manuel Alejandro Hernández Giuliani."
- Slides support the speaker, not replace them — speaker notes for what to say
- Coordinate with Priscila (content) and Dorcas (brand compliance)
