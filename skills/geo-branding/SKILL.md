# GEO Branding

Implement Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) — optimizing content for AI-powered
search engines (Google SGE, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Bing Copilot) — ensuring brand
visibility when users ask AI for answers instead of clicking links.

## What problem this solves
Traditional SEO optimizes for link-based search; GEO optimizes for answer-based
search. When users ask ChatGPT "what's the best data architecture?" instead of
Googling, your brand needs to be in that answer. GEO ensures your content is
cited, referenced, and recommended by AI search engines.

## When to use
Alongside traditional SEO. When AI search traffic becomes significant (> 5%
of total). When competitors appear in AI answers and you don't. When creating
authoritative, citation-worthy content.

## Input
Content to optimize, target AI platforms, competitor analysis.

## Output
A GEO strategy with structured data, entity optimization, citation engineering,
and content formatting optimized for AI extraction and citation.

## Steps
1. Research how AI search platforms cite your domain currently (are you mentioned?)
2. Implement structured data: Schema.org `Article`, `FAQ`, `HowTo`, `Organization`
3. Optimize entity linking: connect content to Wikipedia/Wikidata entities
4. Format content for AI extraction:
   - Use Q&A format for likely queries
   - Include clear definitions, statistics, and original research
   - Cite authoritative sources (AI trusts well-cited content)
5. Build author authority: prominent bio, credentials, publication history
6. Maintain content freshness: update dates, refresh statistics quarterly
7. Monitor: track mentions in AI search results monthly

## Rules
- GEO is complementary to SEO, not a replacement — optimize for both
- Structured data is non-negotiable — AI parses schema.org for facts
- Entity authority: link to established knowledge bases (Wikipedia, Wikidata)
- Q&A format: "What is X?" → H2 question + direct answer in first paragraph
- Unique, citable content: original research, benchmarks, and statistics
- Author authority: visible credentials + publication history on every page
- Monitor AI search traffic separately from traditional search traffic
